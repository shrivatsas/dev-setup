# Question Bank

Ask only what is needed next. Prefer leading questions over open-ended ones.

## Outcome

- What business result are we trying to achieve?
- How will we know this worked?
- What happens if we do nothing?

## Users and workflow

- Who is the primary user?
- What is the current workflow?
- Where does the current process break down?

## Scope and constraints

- What is in scope for this phase?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- What deadline matters most?
- Which constraint is hardest: cost, time, scale, reliability, compliance?

## System shape

- What exists today that we can reuse?
- What must integrate with this system?
- Where is data stored and who owns it?
- What are the likely failure modes?

## Decision framing

- If we optimize for speed, what do we give up?
- If we optimize for simplicity, what do we risk?
- If we optimize for scale, what becomes more complex?
- Which tradeoff matters most right now?
